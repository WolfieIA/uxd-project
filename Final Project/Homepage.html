PARADISE IN THE CITY

About Us

The sun streams in through the ceiling windows to the floor. Your request will be met with tea to fresh seafood. The Sunny Coastal is welcoming and pleasant, yet unmistakably nostalgic. Every design element was chosen with care to capture the playfulness and thoughtful look of childhood. This is your urban retreat, tucked away but only minutes from Singapore's excitement and delights.

Amenities

Gym

The Sunny Coastal Hotel's gym is outfitted with cutting-edge TechnoGym machines that are integrated with individual TV sets and cutting-edge cardiovascular monitors so you can enjoy your workout and track your activity levels.

Swimming pool and Spa

The award-winning Sunny Coastal Hotel's Spa at the Sunny Coastal Hotel Singapore is a tranquil haven in a setting that exudes timeless elegance.

Lobby Lounge

Ballroom

Bar
