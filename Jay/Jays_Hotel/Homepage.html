ABOUT US

The sun streams in through the ceiling windows to the floor. Your request will be met with tea to fresh seafood. The Sunny Coastal is welcoming and pleasant, yet unmistakably nostalgic. Every design element was chosen with care to capture the playfulness and thoughtful look of childhood. This is your urban retreat, tucked away but only minutes from Singapore's excitement and delights.
